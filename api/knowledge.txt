Stenoip Company Knowledge Base Summary

**Overview & Philosophy**
Stenoip Company is a tech company located in North America. Founded in 2021 by a young developer, the platform is pronounced "Stick-noh-ikp". .

The company's core philosophy prioritizes user privacy over profit, explicitly stating they do not collect personal info, track users across sites, or sell data. They position themselves as a "homemade" alternative to big tech monopolies like Google, often critiquing modern "Zero-Click" search trends in their "StenoBlogs."

**Web Portal StenoipKonnect**
* formerly Stenoip Wonder Computer.
* *URL:** https://stenoip.github.io
* **Function:** A customizable dashboard where users add "tiles" for favorite sites, replacing traditional bookmarks.
* **Space Hub:** A dedicated section for astronomy and space exploration news, kept separate from the utility portal to ensure focus.
 * The current platform version is **1.36 (© 2026). It is the first site made my Stenoip Company**

**Browser: Ringzauber**
* **Current Version:** **1.6** (Previous: 1.5, 1.4).
* **Technology:** Built using Python (PyQt5, Flask, QSS).
* **Key Features:**
    * **Praterich Plus:** An integrated AI assistant for browser control and task automation.
    * **Privacy:** History is never saved.
    * **v1.6 Updates:** AI Capture, Camera/Microphone support, native Oodles integration, and the ability to choose a custom search engine (removing the strict dependency on SearXNG).
* **Compatibility:** Windows 7, 8.1, 10, and 11. (Windows XP unsupported).
* **Variants:** " Ringzauber 1.6 Mini" and "Ringzauber Penguin" (for Chromebooks).
* **URL for download:** https://stenoip.github.io/ringzauber

**Search Engine: Oodles Metasearch**
* **Launch Date:** October 19, 2025.
* **URL:** https://stenoip.github.io/oodles
* **Type:** Metasearch engine aggregating results from **Bing, Brave, and Yahoo**.
* **Features:**
    * **AI Synthesis:** Summarizes results and detects tools (Calculator, Translator, Color Picker) via Praterich.
    * **Deep Image Crawler:** Extracts 100-400 images per query.
    * **Index Generator:** Allows users to generate and download their own `index.json` datasets.
    * **World Search:** A 3D interactive mode where search results appear as buildings in a virtual world.
    * **Legacy Support:** functional on Internet Explorer 11.

**Artificial Intelligence: Praterich**
* **Model:** Powered by **Qwen** (chosen over GPT-4 for cost-efficiency).
***URL:** https://stenoip.github.io/praterich
* **Capabilities:**
    * **Chat:** General conversational assistant.
    * **Tools:** Praterich Voice (real-time recognition), Praterich Translator (20+ languages), and experimental Image Creation.
    * **Music:** "StenoSongs" – AI-generated tracks including titles like *oodles.mp3*, *ringzauber.mp3*, and *swc_groove.mp3*.
